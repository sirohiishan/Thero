1. Introduction

Welcome to Thero! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our productivity application. By using Thero, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Important: Thero is a completely local application. All your data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any data on remote servers. We do not use analytics, tracking, or any third-party services.

2. Data Collected

Personal Information

  • Display Name: You can optionally set a display name in the app settings. This is stored locally on your device only and is used solely for personalization within the app.
  • No Email Collection: We do not collect or require email addresses
  • No Authentication: The app does not require account creation, login, or any authentication credentials
  • No User Identification: We do not collect any information that can identify you personally

Task and Content Data

  • Task Content: The tasks and reminders you create in the app, including task titles
  • Task Metadata: Task completion status, creation dates, scheduling information, optional time settings, and priority levels
  • Task Priority: Priority numbers (1 = highest priority, 2 = second priority, 3 = third priority, etc.) assigned to tasks for ordering and organization. Tasks are automatically sorted by priority level, with higher priority tasks (lower numbers) appearing first in the list. When you add a new task, it automatically receives the next available priority number. When you change a task's priority, other tasks automatically adjust their priorities to maintain proper ordering. Priority numbers are displayed next to task titles throughout the app and widgets (e.g., "1.", "2.", "3.")
  • Task Dates: Dates you assign to tasks for organization and scheduling
  • Task Times: Optional time settings for tasks (in "h:mm a" format) used for scheduling notifications
  • Calendar Data: Your selected dates, task organization, and scheduling preferences
  • Productivity Data: Monthly productivity scores calculated locally based on task completion statistics, monthly task goals, and productivity analytics displayed in widgets and monthly views
  • Monthly Goals: Your selected monthly task goal (stored as an integer value) used for productivity tracking

All task data is stored locally on your device only using iOS UserDefaults.

Local Device Data

  • Widget Data: Task lists with priority numbers, productivity scores, task statistics, and completion states cached locally in iOS App Groups (identifier: group.sirohiishan.Moro) for widget functionality. This includes data for small and medium home screen widgets, and lock screen accessoryInline widget. Tasks are sorted by priority in widgets and display priority numbers next to task titles
  • Widget Interaction Data: Task completion toggles from widgets, grace period timestamps (5-second undo period for widget interactions), and pending sync information stored locally
  • Notification Data: Local notification settings, scheduled task reminders, daily motivational notifications (9 AM for all tasks, 9 PM for remaining tasks), and interactive notification action preferences stored on your device using iOS UserNotifications framework
  • Background Task Data: Cached task data for background app refresh and widget updates, stored locally in App Groups
  • App Preferences: User interface preferences, settings, and onboarding completion status stored locally on your device
  • Deep Link Data: Deep link handling for widget interactions (moro://addtask for adding tasks, moro://monthview for monthly productivity view) - processed locally only
  • Task History Data: Historical task data with filtering capabilities (All Time, Past, Upcoming, Custom date range) and search functionality - all stored locally

3. Purpose of Collection

Core App Functionality

  • Task Management: Store and manage your tasks locally on your device with support for dates, optional times, and priority levels. Tasks are automatically sorted by priority (with priority 1 being the highest priority and appearing at the top of the list). New tasks automatically receive the next available priority number. When you change a task's priority, the system automatically adjusts other tasks' priorities to maintain proper ordering. Priority numbers are displayed next to task titles (e.g., "1.", "2.", "3.") in the main task list, task history view, and widgets
  • Local Notifications: Send you reminders for scheduled tasks with specific times using iOS local notifications. For tasks with times, individual notifications are scheduled. For date-only tasks, daily motivational notifications are scheduled at 9 AM (showing all tasks for the day) and 9 PM (showing remaining/incomplete tasks). Interactive notification actions include: Mark Complete, Remind in 1 Hour, Remind in Morning (8:30 AM), Remind in Afternoon (12:30 PM), and Remind at Night (9:00 PM)
  • Widget Functionality: Display task lists with priority numbers, productivity statistics, and task completion status on your device's home screen (small and medium sizes) and lock screen (accessoryInline) with interactive task completion directly from home screen widgets. Tasks in widgets are sorted by priority and display priority numbers (e.g., "1.", "2.", "3.") next to task titles
  • Interactive Home Screen Widgets: Allow you to toggle task completion directly from home screen widgets without opening the app, with a 5-second grace period for undo. Task completion changes are synced back to local storage
  • Lock Screen Widget: Displays daily task count above the time on your device's lock screen, automatically updating to show incomplete tasks for the current day
  • Background Updates: Background app refresh and processing to keep widgets synchronized with local data. Background tasks explicitly do not require network connectivity
  • Calendar Integration: Organize tasks by date with visual calendar view for date selection and provide calendar views with monthly productivity tracking
  • Productivity Tracking: Calculate and display monthly productivity scores, task completion statistics, and productivity analytics locally. Productivity scores are calculated as: (completed tasks / total tasks) Ã— 100
  • Monthly Goals: Set and track monthly task goals (options: 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 40, 50 tasks per month) for productivity motivation
  • Task History: View historical tasks with filtering options (All Time, Past, Upcoming, Custom date range) and search functionality - all data stored and processed locally
  • Deep Linking: Support for deep links from widgets to add tasks (moro://addtask) or view monthly productivity (moro://monthview) - processed locally only

Technical Operations

  • Local Storage: All data is stored securely on your device using iOS UserDefaults and App Groups (group.sirohiishan.Moro) for sharing data between the main app and widget extensions
  • Data Protection: Your data is protected by iOS device encryption and security features, including App Group sandboxing
  • Data Filtering: The app automatically filters tasks when loading data to keep only tasks from the last 60 days and next 365 days in active memory for optimal performance. Tasks outside this range remain in storage but are not loaded into active memory
  • Input Sanitization: Task titles are sanitized to prevent security issues (HTML entities are escaped)
  • No Network Transmission: No data is transmitted from your device to any remote servers. The app works completely offline

User Experience

  • Personalization: Customize the app experience based on your preferences stored locally (display name, monthly goals)
  • Settings Management: Update your name, set monthly goals, and manage app settings - all stored locally
  • Onboarding: Track onboarding completion status to show appropriate screens on first launch
  • App Reset: Ability to reset the app and clear all local data

Tasks outside this range remain in storage but are not loaded into active memory
