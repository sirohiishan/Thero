Getting Started

Welcome to Thero

Thero is a local-only productivity app that helps you manage tasks with priorities, track your productivity, and stay organized. All your data is stored exclusively on your device for complete privacy.

Quick Start: When you first open Thero, you'll see an introduction screen. Tap "Get Started" to begin creating and managing your tasks. No account creation or login required!

Interactive Tutorial

Thero includes a step-by-step interactive tutorial that guides you through key features:

  • Step 1: Learn how to add your first task
  • Step 2: Understand the task creation screen
  • Step 3: Set task dates for better organization
  • Step 4: Add specific times for task reminders
  • Step 5: Manage task priorities
  • Step 6: Learn how to remove date/time selections
  • Step 7: Edit and delete tasks

The tutorial appears automatically on your first use and can be replayed anytime from Settings → Tutorial. Each step highlights important features with visual emphasis to help you learn by doing.

Task Management

Creating Tasks

  • Tap the "+" button or the "Add Task" button to create a new task
  • Enter your task title
  • Optionally set a date and time for your task
  • Tasks are automatically assigned priority numbers (1, 2, 3...) based on when you create them

Task Priorities

Tasks in Thero use a priority system where lower numbers mean higher priority:

  • Priority 1 is the highest priority and appears at the top of your list
  • Tasks are automatically sorted by priority, with incomplete tasks shown first
  • When you complete a task, it automatically moves to the bottom with the lowest priority
  • You can change a task's priority by editing it - other tasks will automatically adjust
  • Priority numbers are displayed next to task titles (e.g., "1.", "2.", "3.")
  • Note: Completed tasks cannot have their priority edited

Editing and Deleting Tasks

  • Edit: Tap on any task to edit its title, date, time, or priority
  • Delete: Swipe left on a task to reveal the delete option, or delete from the edit screen
  • Complete: Tap the circle next to a task to mark it as complete

Task Dates and Times

  • You can assign dates to tasks for better organization
  • Optionally set specific times for tasks (in "h:mm a" format, e.g., "9:00 AM")
  • Use the calendar view to navigate between dates and see tasks for specific days
  • Tasks with times will receive individual notification reminders
  • Tasks with only dates (no time) will be included in daily motivational notifications

Widgets

Home Screen Widgets

Thero offers interactive widgets for your home screen in two sizes:

  • Small Widget: Displays your task count, next task (with priority number), and a quick add button. Shows the first incomplete task with its priority number (e.g., "1.")
  • Medium Widget: Shows your task count and up to 4 tasks with priority numbers, completion status, and a quick add button. Tasks are sorted by priority (lower number = higher priority)
  • Interactive: Tap task circles directly in the widget to mark tasks complete/incomplete (iOS 17+). The small widget allows toggling the first task; the medium widget allows toggling any visible task
  • Tasks in widgets are sorted by priority and show priority numbers (e.g., "1.", "2.", "3.") next to task titles
  • There's a 5-second grace period after toggling - tap again to undo within 5 seconds
  • Widgets update automatically throughout the day and refresh at midnight to show the correct day's tasks

Lock Screen Widget

  • The lock screen widget displays your daily task count above the time (accessoryInline format)
  • It automatically shows the number of incomplete tasks for the current day (e.g., "4 Tasks")
  • The widget updates automatically throughout the day and refreshes at midnight
  • To add: Long-press your lock screen, tap "Customize", then add Thero's "Moro Lock Screen" widget

Adding Widgets

  1. Long-press on your home screen or lock screen
  2. Tap the "+" button in the top-left corner
  3. Search for "Thero"
  4. Select the widget size you want
  5. Tap "Add Widget"
  6. Position it where you'd like on your screen

Notifications

Task Reminders

  • Tasks with Times: Receive individual notification reminders at the specified time. Notifications are scheduled for the next 30 days in advance
  • Tasks with Dates Only: Included in daily motivational notifications
  • Daily Notifications:
    • 9:00 AM - Shows all tasks scheduled for the day. These notifications are scheduled 7 days in advance
    • 9:00 PM - Shows remaining/incomplete tasks for the day. These notifications are scheduled 7 days in advance

Interactive Notification Actions

When you receive a task notification, you can:

  • Mark Complete: Instantly mark the task as done
  • Remind in 1 Hour: Get another reminder in one hour
  • Remind in Morning: Get a reminder at 8:30 AM
  • Remind in Afternoon: Get a reminder at 12:30 PM
  • Remind at Night: Get a reminder at 9:00 PM

Managing Notifications

  • Notifications are managed through your device's Settings app
  • Go to Settings → Notifications → Thero to enable or disable notifications
  • Thero uses provisional notifications, which work quietly without requiring explicit permission
  • All notifications are generated locally on your device

Productivity Tracking

Monthly Productivity Score

  • View your monthly productivity in the "My Month" view
  • Productivity score is calculated as: (completed tasks / total tasks) × 100
  • Track your progress throughout the month
  • View task completion statistics and analytics
  • Navigate between months using the arrow buttons to view historical productivity data
  • See detailed stats: Total Tasks, Tasks Done, and Still In Progress

Task History

  • View all your tasks with filtering options
  • Filter by: All Time, Past, Upcoming, or Custom date range
  • Search functionality to quickly find specific tasks by title
  • Tasks are grouped by date with clear date headers
  • Swipe left on any task to reveal the delete option
  • Tap any task to edit its details (title, date, time, priority)
  • Tap the circle next to a task to toggle completion status
  • All historical data is stored locally on your device
  • Note: The main app interface shows tasks from the last 60 days and next 365 days for optimal performance, but Task History can access all stored tasks

Settings

Display Name

  • Set a custom display name in Settings
  • Tap the pencil icon next to your name to edit it
  • Your name is stored locally and used for personalization

Tutorial

  • Replay the interactive tutorial anytime from Settings → Tutorial
  • The tutorial will guide you through all key features step-by-step
  • Replaying the tutorial does not delete any of your data - it only resets tutorial progress

App Reset

Warning: This action cannot be undone!

  • Resetting the app will permanently delete all your tasks, settings, and data
  • This includes: tasks, widget data, notifications, display name, onboarding status, and tutorial progress
  • To reset: Go to Settings → Reset App → Confirm

Troubleshooting

Widgets Not Updating

  • Make sure Background App Refresh is enabled for Thero in Settings → Thero → Background App Refresh
  • Try removing and re-adding the widget
  • Restart your device if widgets still don't update

Widget Buttons Not Working

  • Interactive widgets require iOS 17.0 or later
  • Check your iOS version in Settings → General → About

Notifications Not Appearing

  • Check that notifications are enabled in Settings → Notifications → Thero
  • Make sure your device is not in Do Not Disturb mode
  • Verify that tasks have dates and/or times set
  • Check that your device's system time is correct

Tasks Not Syncing Between App and Widgets

  • Widget interactions update immediately in the widget
  • Changes sync to the main app when it's running
  • If the app is closed, changes will sync when you next open it
  • There's a 5-second grace period after widget interactions - you can tap again to undo

App Performance

  • Thero automatically filters tasks to show only the last 60 days and next 365 days in the main interface for optimal performance
  • Tasks outside this range remain in storage but aren't loaded into active memory in the main view
  • Task History view can access all stored tasks regardless of date range
  • If the app feels slow, try closing and reopening it
  • All data is stored locally, so no internet connection is required

Privacy and Data

Important: Thero is a completely local application. All your data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any data on remote servers. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

  • All tasks, settings, and data are stored locally on your device
  • Data is protected by iOS device encryption
  • No cloud sync or backup services are used
  • If you delete the app, all data is removed from your device
